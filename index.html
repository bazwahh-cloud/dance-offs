Frequently Asked Questions

< How do I enter to win? >

Every paid vote counts as one entry into that months Prize Draw. The more votes you cast, the more chances you have to win!

< How is the winning ticket drawn? >

At the end of each month, all valid paid votes are placed into a randomised draw. One lucky entry is selected at random using a secure, automated system.

< What is this month's prize? >

This month’s prize is shown on the Prizes page.

< What happens if I can’t attend the event? >

You can nominate someone else to attend in your place, or choose a cash alternative.

< How will I be notified if I win? >

Winners are contacted directly via the email address used during voting. Make sure your email is correct when you cast your vote!

< How do I contact Dance-Offs? >

You can reach us anytime using the contact form at the bottom of this page. Enter your name, email, and message — we’ll get back to you as soon as possible!
