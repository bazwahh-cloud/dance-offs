Welcome to Dance-Offs

This site is your hub for all things Dance-Offs — battles, DJs, voting, and more. Use the navigation bar above to explore each section. Whether you're here to compete, vote, or just enjoy the show, everything you need is one click away.

How to Navigate

  • Dancers — Browse the dancers who compete in the battles.
  • Battle — Load two YouTube videos and compare them side-by-side.
  • DJs — Explore DJ mixes and SoundCloud tracks.
  • Vote — Watch the current matchup and cast your vote.
  • Merch — Grab official Dance-Offs gear.

New battles and mixes are added regularly, so check back often. Have fun exploring — and may the best dancer win.
