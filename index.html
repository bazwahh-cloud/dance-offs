Frequently Asked Questions

For Voters

< How do I enter to win? >

Every paid vote counts as one entry into that month's Prize Draw. The more votes you cast, the more chances you have to win!

< How is the winning ticket drawn? >

At the end of each month, all valid paid votes are placed into a randomised draw. One lucky entry is selected at random using a secure, automated system.

< What is this month's prize? >

This month’s prize is shown on the Prize page.

< How will I know if I win? >

Winners are contacted via the email address used during voting. Make sure your email is correct when you cast your vote!

< Can I vote from outside the UK? >

Yes. Dance‑Offs accepts votes from most countries worldwide. Prize winners outside the UK may receive a cash alternative if travel is not possible.

For Dancers

< Can I use this site for free? >

Yes. All dancers can use Dance‑Offs for free. You can appear in battles, receive votes, and share your profile without paying anything.

< How do I upload a video? >

Paste your YouTube link, in YouTube ensure you have set the video visibility to "Public". Make sure your video is allowed to play on other websites. YouTube has a setting called “Allow embedding”. This must be turned ON.

< How do I get to the top of the leaderboard? >

The leaderboard is based on votes. The more votes you receive, the higher you climb. Sharing your battle link on social media is the best way to boost your ranking.

< What happens if I get votes? >

Votes increase your position in the battle rankings. If you win your battle, you move closer to the monthly finals and gain more visibility across the platform.

For DJs

< Can I use the site for free? >

Yes. Uploading your mix and appearing on the DJ leaderboard is completely free. There are no fees to take part.

< How do I upload a mix? >

Go to the DJs page, enter your mix details, choose your MP3 file (around 10 mins long), and press "Upload Mix". Your track will appear on the site once it’s processed.
