Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter to win?

Every paid vote you cast counts as one entry into the monthly Prize Draw. The more votes you cast, the more entries you have.

How is the winning ticket drawn?

At the end of each month, all valid paid votes are placed into a randomised draw. One entry is selected at random using a secure, automated system.

What is this month's prize?

This month’s prize is shown on the Prizes page. It updates monthly.

What happens if I can’t attend the event?

You can nominate someone else to attend in your place, or choose an alternative reward if one is available.

How will I be notified if I win?

Winners are contacted directly via the email address used during voting. Make sure your email is correct when you cast your vote.
